Frequently Asked Questions

What types of auto insurance do you offer?

We offer liability, collision, comprehensive, uninsured motorist, and personal injury protection (PIP) coverage.

What is the minimum coverage required?

The minimum coverage required depends on your state, but it typically includes liability insurance for bodily injury and property damage.

How can I file a claim?

You can file a claim by calling our 24/7 claims hotline, using our mobile app, or visiting our website.

What documents do I need to file a claim?

To file a claim, you will need your policy number, a police report (if applicable), and any evidence such as photos of the damage.

Does my policy cover rental cars?

Rental car coverage is available as an add-on. Check your policy or contact us to see if you have this coverage.

Are there discounts available?

Yes, we offer various discounts, including safe driver discounts, multi-policy discounts, and good student discounts.

What is collision coverage?

Collision coverage helps pay for repairs to your vehicle after an accident, regardless of who is at fault.

What is comprehensive coverage?

Comprehensive coverage protects against non-collision incidents, such as theft, vandalism, or natural disasters.

What happens if I drive someone else’s car?

Typically, the car owner’s insurance is primary. However, your policy may provide secondary coverage in certain situations.

How can I update my policy?

You can update your policy online, through our mobile app, or by calling customer service.

Can I cancel my policy at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your policy at any time by contacting our customer service team.

What is a deductible?

A deductible is the amount you agree to pay out-of-pocket before your insurance covers the rest of the claim.

Does my policy cover towing?

Towing coverage is available as an add-on. Check your policy to see if you have this feature.

What is uninsured motorist coverage?

Uninsured motorist coverage protects you if you are in an accident caused by a driver who does not have insurance.

How are premiums calculated?

Premiums are calculated based on factors such as your driving history, location, vehicle type, and coverage level.

Can I add another driver to my policy?

Yes, you can add another driver to your policy by contacting customer service or updating your policy online.

What should I do if I am in an accident?

Ensure everyone is safe, call the police, exchange information with the other driver, and contact us to start a claim.

Is roadside assistance included?

Roadside assistance is available as an optional add-on. Check your policy to see if you have this feature.

Do you offer gap insurance?

Yes, gap insurance is available and covers the difference between your car’s value and the amount you owe on it.

How can I lower my premium?

You can lower your premium by maintaining a clean driving record, bundling policies, and taking advantage of discounts.

What is an SR-22?

An SR-22 is a certificate of financial responsibility required by some states for high-risk drivers.

Do you offer coverage for custom parts and equipment?

Yes, we offer custom parts and equipment coverage as an add-on to your policy.

What is personal injury protection (PIP)?

PIP covers medical expenses and lost wages for you and your passengers, regardless of who is at fault.

Can I switch my policy to a different car?

Yes, you can transfer your policy to a new car by updating your policy details with us.
