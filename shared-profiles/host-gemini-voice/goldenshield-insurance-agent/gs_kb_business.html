Frequently Asked Questions

What types of business insurance do you offer?

We offer general liability, professional liability, commercial property, workers' compensation, and business interruption insurance.

What is general liability insurance?

General liability insurance covers your business against claims of bodily injury, property damage, and personal injury.

Do I need workers' compensation insurance?

Workers' compensation insurance is required in most states if you have employees. It covers work-related injuries and illnesses.

What is business interruption insurance?

Business interruption insurance helps cover lost income and operating expenses if your business is temporarily closed due to a covered event.

Does my policy cover natural disasters?

Coverage for natural disasters like earthquakes or floods depends on the policy. Separate policies may be required for these events.

How can I file a claim?

You can file a claim online, through our mobile app, or by contacting our claims department directly at 1-800-GOLDEN.

What is a business owner’s policy (BOP)?

A BOP combines general liability, commercial property, and business interruption insurance into one convenient package.

Can I customize my policy?

Yes, our policies are customizable to meet the specific needs of your business. Contact us to tailor your coverage.

What is professional liability insurance?

Professional liability insurance, also known as errors and omissions (E&O) insurance, protects against claims of negligence or mistakes in professional services.

Does my policy cover cyberattacks?

Cyber liability insurance is available to protect your business from data breaches and cyberattacks. Check if it’s included in your policy.

What is commercial property insurance?

Commercial property insurance covers physical assets such as buildings, equipment, and inventory against loss or damage.

Are home-based businesses covered?

Homeowners insurance may not cover business-related risks. A separate business insurance policy is recommended.

How are premiums calculated?

Premiums are based on factors such as business size, industry, location, and claims history.

Can I bundle multiple policies?

Yes, bundling policies can save you money and simplify your insurance management. Contact us for bundling options.

What is product liability insurance?

Product liability insurance protects your business from claims related to injuries or damages caused by your products.

How can I reduce my premium costs?

You can reduce premium costs by implementing safety measures, maintaining a clean claims history, and bundling policies.

What is employers' liability insurance?

Employers' liability insurance protects your business from claims by employees for work-related injuries not covered by workers' compensation.

What should I do in case of a loss?

Document the damage, report the loss to our claims department, and take steps to prevent further damage if safe to do so.

Is equipment breakdown covered?

Equipment breakdown insurance can be added to cover repairs or replacements for essential business equipment.

Do you cover employee theft?

Employee theft can be covered under a fidelity bond or crime insurance policy. Contact us for details.

What is inland marine insurance?

Inland marine insurance covers goods and equipment in transit or stored offsite.

How do I update my policy?

You can update your policy online, through our mobile app, or by contacting your agent directly.
