Frequently Asked Questions

What types of life insurance policies do you offer?

We offer term life, whole life, and universal life insurance policies to meet your needs and budget.

How can I file a claim?

You can file a claim by visiting our website, calling our customer service hotline at 1-800-GOLDEN, or contacting your insurance agent directly.

What documents are required to process a claim?

Generally, we require a completed claim form, the policyholder's death certificate, and proof of identity for the claimant. Additional documents may be requested based on your case.

Can I update my policy beneficiaries?

Yes, you can update your beneficiaries at any time by filling out the beneficiary change form on our website or contacting our customer service team.

Do you offer online account management?

Yes, you can manage your account, view policy details, and make payments through our secure online portal.

How can I get a quote for a policy?

You can get a free quote by using our online quote tool or by contacting one of our licensed insurance agents.

What is the claims process timeline?

Claims are typically processed within 10-15 business days after receiving all necessary documents.

Are there any exclusions to my policy?

Yes, exclusions may include suicide within the first two years, certain risky activities, or misrepresentation during application. Review your policy for detailed information.

How can I contact customer support?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 via phone at 1-800-GOLDEN, email at support@goldenshield.com, or live chat on our website.

What payment options are available for policy premiums?

We accept credit/debit cards, ACH transfers, checks, and online payments through our secure portal.

Can I change the coverage amount of my policy?

Yes, you can adjust your coverage amount based on your changing needs, subject to underwriting approval.

What happens if I miss a premium payment?

If you miss a payment, a grace period of 30 days is provided. Failure to pay within the grace period may result in policy lapse.

How does cash value work in a whole life policy?

The cash value accumulates over time and can be accessed via loans or withdrawals, but it reduces the death benefit if not repaid.

Do you offer rider options for policies?

Yes, we provide rider options such as accidental death, waiver of premium, and critical illness riders to enhance your coverage.

Is medical underwriting required for all policies?

Not all policies require medical underwriting. Simplified issue or guaranteed issue policies may not require a medical exam.

How can I reinstate a lapsed policy?

You can reinstate a lapsed policy within a certain time frame by paying the overdue premiums and possibly providing proof of insurability.

What is the difference between term and whole life insurance?

Term life provides coverage for a specific period, while whole life offers lifelong protection with a cash value component.

Are premium rates guaranteed?

Premium rates for term policies are usually guaranteed for the term duration. Whole life policies have fixed premiums for life.

Can I cancel my policy at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your policy anytime by notifying us. Refunds for unearned premiums depend on policy terms.

What happens if I outlive my term life policy?

If you outlive the term, the policy ends. Some term policies offer conversion to whole life or renewal options.
